SPACE MARIO

Overview
The game is an infinite platformer, the platforms come at you and you just jump to try and stay on top of them.

How To Play
 - Press Up arrow to jump
 - Press P to pause
 - If you lost or won, you can press R to restart the level

Win Condition
 - as the platforms scroll by, you get 1 point
 - if the moon or sun passes by, you get 10 points
 - if score hits 50, you win

Lose Condition
 - if you go off the bottom of the screen, you lose
